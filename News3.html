The Battle Between Biden and Trump

The political battle between Joe Biden and Donald Trump has been one of the most contentious in modern American history. As the current President, Joe Biden represents the Democratic Party, while Donald Trump, the former President, is the face of the Republican Party. The two have starkly different views on many issues, including the economy, healthcare, and foreign policy.

Joe Biden

Joe Biden, the 46th President of the United States, has focused on tackling the COVID-19 pandemic, rebuilding the economy, and addressing climate change. His administration has passed significant legislation, including the American Rescue Plan and the Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act.

Donald Trump

Donald Trump, the 45th President of the United States, is known for his "America First" policies, which aimed to reduce immigration, renegotiate trade deals, and withdraw from international agreements. His time in office was marked by significant tax cuts and deregulation efforts.
