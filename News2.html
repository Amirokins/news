Learn about the impact of global warming on various animal species and what we can do to help.

Impact of Global Warming

Global warming is causing significant changes in the environment, which affect animal habitats and survival.

  • Rising temperatures
  • Melting ice caps
  • Extreme weather events

Animals Affected by Global Warming

Polar Bear

Polar bears are losing their sea ice habitat, which they rely on for hunting seals.

Coral Reefs

Warmer ocean temperatures are causing coral bleaching and the decline of reef ecosystems.

Tigers

Changing weather patterns and habitat loss are threatening tiger populations.

Solutions to Combat Global Warming

There are various ways we can help mitigate the effects of global warming and protect wildlife.

  • Reduce carbon footprint
  • Support conservation efforts
  • Promote renewable energy
